The capybara is the largest living rodent. Click each picture below to learn more about these animals.

Capybaras are talented swimmers. Their webbed toes and long fur make them perfectly adapted to life in the water.
Source: National Geographic
Image source: Pixabay

Capybaras are herbivores. They enjoy a diet of grass and aquatic plants. Interestingly, capybaras consume their own feces as a means of collecting bacteria to aid digestion of the tough vegetal fibers.
Source: Rainforest Alliance
Image source: Unsplash

Although the capybara appears cute and cuddly, these animals grow to an astounding size. In length, they range from 39-51 inches (3-4 feet). The rodent can weigh as much as 200 pounds.
Source: Livescience
Image source: Unsplash
